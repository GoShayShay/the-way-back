Something New

Did you know that in the 80s icon Tina Turner shot at and grazed the scalp of Randy Jackson? That's right!

According to the report, Tina was advising Bernadette on her relationship with Jackson.

“Bernadette, let me tell you something, you’ve got yourself an Ike Turner, You better get rid of him. I know you’re scared. I know what it’s like, Bernadette. It’s best for you if you stay here tonight. Just make sure you get rid of that Randy Jackson first thing tomorrow.”

When Randy dropped by Turner’s home to try to convince Bernadette to leave with him, he became furious when no one would answer the door. He then allegedly hurled a flower-pot through the window — and that’s when all hell broke loose.
